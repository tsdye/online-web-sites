ANTH 464

Class presentation

[Dr. Thomas S. Dye] · 2018.10.14

Your research presentation should not be a simple “replay” of your topic but should be re-designed so that you

  • spend some time talking spontaneously
  • employ more than one medium (such as slides)
  • engage your classmates in discussion

A successful presentation will focus on one part of the research, without trying to cover all of the research in your paper. You will have about 15–20 minutes to make your presentation; this will include time devoted to questions and answers.

Your presentation will be graded separately from your paper.
