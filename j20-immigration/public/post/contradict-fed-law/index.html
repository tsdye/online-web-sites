Question

Doesn’t a state sanctuary law contradict federal law?

Answer

Hawaii’s proposed sanctuary state bill does not conflict with federal law because it allows federal immigration efforts to continue. Constitutional experts agree that the provisions of Hawaii’s proposed sanctuary state bill are legal. None of its provisions have been found by any court to be in violation of federal law or the U.S. Constitution.
