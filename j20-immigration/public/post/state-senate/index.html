Question:

Did my State Senator support the Ho`okipa Bill during the 2018 legislative session?

Background:

Answer:

Support for the 2018 Ho`okipa Bill was strong in the Senate, where it was introduced by Senators Rhoads, Chang, and Taniguchi. Twenty-two senators consistently voted for the bill. Senator Mercado Kim had reservations, and Senators Gabbard and Riviere voted against the bill.
